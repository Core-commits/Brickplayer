 BrickPlayer Extension Template.

A tool to complement your node-hill game with brickplayer with custom meshes, skyboxes and more.   

Installation Instructions:

Download the BrickPlayer server script by clicking on the button below. Next, open the script and set the resourcePack you can customize from the template. Restart your server, and the resource pack should be sent to all BrickPlayer users.

Features of the Resource Packs

  • [Unstable] Add custom assets.
  • [Stable] Modifying mesh and texture of existing assets
  • [Stable] Changing texture of studs, inlets, and spawnpoints
  • [Stable] Adjusting texture size of studs and inlets
  • [Stable] Adding a skybox
